Jollof Rice Recipes

Description

Jollof rice is a spiced dish, simmered in reduced tomatoes, onions, peppers, and different seasonings depending on where it's made.

Ingredients
  • Rice
  • Tomato paste, onions and sardine
  • Seasonings, sausages and mixed vegetables
  • Chicken
Steps for making Jollof Rice
  1. Per boil rice for 10 minutes and wash
  2. Put on fire with chicken stock and add seasonings
  3. Leave till water in the rice gets dried
  4. Boil the chicken and fry
  5. Turn rice with the seasonings well and viola jollof is ready!
