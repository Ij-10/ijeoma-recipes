Noodles Recipes

Description

Noodles, a cooked egg-and-flour paste prominent in European and Asian cuisine, generally distinguished from pasta by its elongated ribbonlike form.

Ingredients
  • Noodles
  • Chicken
  • Seasonings
  • Mixed Vegetables
Steps for making noodles
  1. Put noodles on fire
  2. Wait for 5 minutes then drain the water
  3. Add Seasonings, chicken and mixed vegetables
  4. Turn noodles well and viola noodles is ready!
